Revitalize, Refresh, Reshape

Our products are tailored to support your unique needs. We carry only the best products that promote the health of your hair and help you to recreate your salon experience at home. MC23 currently offers a range of the finest products: Kerastase and Oribe. Both product lines address specific hair needs. Between Kerastase and Oribe, ranging from light to intense, we have you covered.

Kérastase

Kérastase is our treatment based product. It will revitalize hair at any stage compromise or damage. Kérastase is based on four essential values: performance, expertise, personalization, and beauty.

Oribe

Oribe's products have only the highest grade ingredients and is Sodium Chloride free. Oribe is pure luxury. Feel inspired by products that execute exactly as promised, while conditioning and replenishing your hair.

Christy Stutsman

As the owner of MC23, Christy wants to know what makes you feel beautiful. Her goal is to inspire and create a new perspective of you. Christy trained and continues her education with industry leaders like Vidal Sassoon to hone her technical skills. She also specializes in hair extentions and Keratin Smoothers. Christy loves to be able to gift her clients a better texture or thicker hair. She creates the most natural of hilites to creative placement, boliage, and rich tints. Whether you are looking for an edgy or feminine look, she will lead you in the right direction with your haircut. She holds hairdressing in the highest of regards, and loves her craft.
